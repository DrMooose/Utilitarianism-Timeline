Ethics and Motives: Examining the Six Ethical Philosophies

The six ethical philosophies are an important aspect in analyzing situations and the ethical actions that are behind them. Many pieces of work can ethically be separated into one of these six categories. To be able to understand each philosophy is an asset to any individual looking to find the ethics behind other's actions. This site looks to try and create a place that will examine all six ethical philosophies, each being sectioned off into its own page, with a color that corresponds to the core values of the philosophy.
